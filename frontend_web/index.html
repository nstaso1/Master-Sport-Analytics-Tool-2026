    🧠 How the Readiness Grade is Calculated

    The Mystics Omni-Engine does not use a simple weighted average. It uses a custom client-side Random Forest Regressor to evaluate prospects, simulating how a real scouting department analyzes non-linear data.

    1. Tier Normalization

    Before any math occurs, the player's raw metrics are measured against the Target Baseline for their specific competitive tier (D1, Pro, Minors). This converts their raw data into a standardized 0-100 scale.

    2. The Random Forest Ensemble

    The normalized stats are fed into an ensemble of 50 distinct decision trees. Each tree looks at a random subset of the player's 5 tools (Feature Bagging) and sets random non-linear split thresholds to vote on the player's overall grade. The final Overall Grade is the aggregate average of all 50 trees.

    3. Non-Linear Synergy & Bust Penalties

    Unlike standard linear math, this model captures thresholds perfectly. If a player possesses elite primary traits that cross specific thresholds, the trees award a massive "Synergy Boost" recognizing superstar potential. Conversely, if a fundamental trait drops below a critical threshold, the trees apply a "Bust Penalty" that tanks their grade harder than linear subtraction would.
